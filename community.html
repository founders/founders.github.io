The Community Team focuses on growing the Founders brand. We seek to inform students of the entrepreneurial resources available through our organization. Our team is responsible for all Founders marketing & design material on our social media, newsletter, and website.

How did you hear about Founders? How did you find yourself on this website at all? Chances are, it was all orchestrated by the Community team. This team has specializations in outreach and communications, and it seeks to figure out how the Founders brand can grow and change over time to suit the needs of entrepreneurs. Community is one of the most academically diverse teams in Founders, with designers, computer scientists, business majors, and more, all working together to help Founders improve as an organization.
